ABOUT KHANG

KHANG is a house of Lao textile and modern fashion, founded by a master of ancient silk, and who is also Laoâ€™s leading fashion designer, Viengkham Nanthavongdouangsy. Her works have received many international awards for excellent quality and design.

All textiles of KHANG are produced in the traditional Lao way; the ageless methods of making cloths and natural dyeing have been kept alive here. With the creations of KHANG you will find modern designs that are crafted using the ancient weaving techniques, and only natural materials are selected for its production.

KHANG embraces the sustainability within its work; it contributes creating jobs in different communities - from farmers to weavers and other professions in the textiles and fashion industry.

Handing down the ancient technical know-how of textiles production is an important activity of KHANG. Over the past 20 years, Viengkham has trained many hundreds of people, both locally and internationally, on weaving and natural dyeing, and she has written and published a number of books on Lao textiles.
